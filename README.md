## 💭 The Unfiltered Truth

Most profiles here are highlight reels. Here's the reality check:

**I'm not a smooth talker.**  
I pause, I rethink, sometimes I explain better in Hindi. But I don't hide behind jargon. I value clarity over fancy vocabulary. My channel growth and hackathon leadership prove I can communicate what matters.

**I have a distraction problem: Robotics.**  
I don't just sit at a desk coding—I build things that move. This "distraction" means I understand the full stack: hardware, software, and real-world chaos. I write code that survives outside the IDE.

**I'm addicted to AI tools.**  
I realized handwriting every bracket was ego, not efficiency. AI helped me ship E-Pustakaalay and vibekode.studio in half the time. I'm not a syntax memorizer; I'm a problem solver who uses every tool available.

**I overthink everything.**  
I stare at problem statements longer than most. It kills my speed, but I catch bugs "fast" developers miss. I care deeply about user experience, even the parts no one sees.
